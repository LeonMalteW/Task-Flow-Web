Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application TaskFlow.

2. Overview

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. TaskFlow is designed with a strong focus on privacy and data minimization.

3. Data Collection by TaskFlow

TaskFlow itself does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

All data created within the app (such as tasks or schedules) is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any external servers by the app.

4. Data Processing by Platform Providers

When downloading, installing, or using TaskFlow via the Google Play Store, certain data may be collected and processed by Google as the platform provider.

This may include technical information such as device details, usage data, or diagnostics. This processing is carried out solely by Google and is outside of our control.

For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Since TaskFlow itself does not process personal data, no processing within the meaning of Article 4 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is carried out by the app.

6. Data Sharing

TaskFlow does not share any personal data with third parties.

7. Children’s Privacy

TaskFlow does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is processed by the app, its use is considered safe in this regard.

8. Your Rights

As TaskFlow does not process personal data, there are no user data records held by the app that could be accessed, corrected, or deleted.

For any data processed by Google, please refer directly to Google’s tools and privacy controls.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy if necessary. The latest version will always be available on this page.
